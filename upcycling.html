Zero Waste through Upcycling

The Black Market Clothing Exchange eliminates over 700 lbs of textile waste from Lancaster County landfill every week.

What do we do?

All A & B Quality clothing and accessories for Fair Market Value are reused and recycled. We reuse and recycle all paper & cardboard products such as shoe boxes and tissues paper.

We are a bagless store; we do not use plastic bags.
