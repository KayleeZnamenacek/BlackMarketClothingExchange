How to Sell at The Black Market

Our expert buyers will evaluate your items and offer cash or credit based upon brand, style, and condition.

Bring your clothing items to us!

Clothing brought in to us must meet the requirements below:

  • Laundered and Folded
  • Free of odors, holes, and stains.
  • Brought in a tote with a lid, laundry basket, or box.
  • No loose or overflowing items, trash bags, or hangers please.

Clothing items we do accept are denim, tops, bottoms, button-downs, polos, skirts, dresses, formal, maternity, business, athleisure, jackets, hoodies, hats, shoes, costume jewelry, purses, handbags, and other accessories.
