How to Sell

Our expert buyers will evaluate your items and offer cash or credit based upon brand, style, and condition.

  • Free of odors, holes, and stains.
  • Brought in a tote with a lid, laundry basket, or box.
  • No loose or overflowing items, trash bags, or hangers please.
